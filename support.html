Help & Support

We're here to help with any questions or issues you may have

Frequently Asked Questions

You can purchase tickets through our website or mobile app by following these steps:

  1. Select your departure and arrival locations
  2. Choose your travel date and time
  3. Select the number of passengers
  4. Choose your preferred payment method (Mobile Money, Credit Card, or USSD)
  5. Complete your purchase and receive your e-ticket via SMS or email

Yes, you can cancel or change your ticket up to 1 hour before departure:

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure receive a full refund
  • Cancellations made 1-24 hours before departure receive a 50% refund
  • To change your ticket, cancel the existing one and purchase a new ticket
  • Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days

You can find complete schedules in several ways:

  • On our website's Routes & Schedule page
  • Through our mobile app
  • At any of our bus stations
  • By calling our customer service at +268 2404 0000

Schedules may change during holidays and special events, so please check for updates.

Our real-time tracking is typically accurate within 1-2 minutes:

  • Buses are equipped with GPS tracking devices
  • Location data updates every 30 seconds
  • ETAs are calculated based on current traffic conditions
  • During heavy traffic or road construction, accuracy may be slightly affected

For the most accurate information, check the tracking system close to your departure time.

We accept several convenient payment methods:

  • Mobile Money: MTN Eswatini and Eswatini Mobile
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard
  • USSD: Dial *123*1# and follow prompts
  • Cash: At designated ticket offices

Online purchases require Mobile Money, Card, or USSD payment.

Yes, we operate cross-border routes to South Africa:

  • Manzini → Johannesburg: Daily departures at 6:00 AM and 2:00 PM
  • Mbabane → Nelspruit: Three daily departures (7:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM)
  • Passengers must have valid travel documents
  • Border crossing may add 30-60 minutes to travel time

Please arrive at least 45 minutes before departure for border formalities.

Lost & Found

Report Lost Item

If you've lost an item on one of our buses, please fill out the form below. We'll check our lost and found inventory and contact you if your item is found.

Tips for recovering lost items:

  • Report the loss as soon as possible
  • Provide detailed description of the item
  • Include identifying marks or features
  • Remember the date, time, and route of your trip
